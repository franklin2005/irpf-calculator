## Goal
Ship SSR pages that rank and load fast.

## Rules
- Unique title/description per page.
- Canonical URLs where needed.
- Content must be useful without JavaScript.
- Keep pages lightweight (Ads and analytics come later).

## Verification
- Confirm meta tags render server-side.
- Spot-check Lighthouse locally if possible.
